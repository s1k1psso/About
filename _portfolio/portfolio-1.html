--- title: "Alberta Data Centres Zine" excerpt: "A zine about data centres, oil, militarization, and Alberta.
" collection: portfolio --- A zine about data centres, oil, militarization, and Alberta. Created due to my frustration at the speed and scale at which data centres are being pushed through in the province, with only sporadic resistance efforts. The zine is information heavy, but much of the information has not been brought together in one place before - at least not for within the context of Alberta. What kinds of stories do we tell about data centres - whether to justify them, or to oppose them? What do these narratives include or leave out? The zine is designed to be shared widely, so please get in touch if you would like a printable version. If there are any errors, omissions, or suggestons, please let me know.
