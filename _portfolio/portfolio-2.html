--- title: "Suffield (TTRPG)" excerpt: "A TTPRG based on a true story from Alberta's past. Prairie dogs, detonations, and guerilla warfare
" collection: portfolio --- A TTPRG based on a true story from Alberta's past. On July 17, 1964, the Defence Research Board of Canada - in collaboration with the US and UK militaries - detonated 500 short tonnes of TNT at CFB Suffield (in southern Alberta), named Operation Snowball. It remains the largest man-made & non-accidental explosion in Canadian history. It created a crater 73m across. Rumour has it, it also created a group of unhoused prairie dogs who now hate the international weapons industry. They have decided to take up arms and wage a war of survival, destroying military infrastructure at CFB Suffield to reclaim their home. This version has slight visual modifications, and the added 'story mods' optional section to diversify the gameplay. This was my first time every creating or playing a TTPRG, and I thought that a collective goal worked towards by all players would be a creative twist.
